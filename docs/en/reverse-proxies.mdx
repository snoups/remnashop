<Warning>
    **Warning**
    <br />
    Remnashop and its components do not support being served on a sub-path.
    <br />
    <br />
    (e.g. <Badge color="yellow">location /remnashop</Badge>,{" "}
    <Badge color="yellow">location /remnawave</Badge>)
</Warning>

<Note>
    **Note**
    <br />
    **SSH Port Forwarding** is not supported. Use only one of the reverse
    proxies listed below.
</Note>

<Tip>
    **Tip**
    <br />
    You need to have a registered domain name to continue.
</Tip>
